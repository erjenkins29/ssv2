A vaild month is an integer or string in the format 'YYYYmm'.
The length should be 6. The first two digits should be '20'.
The last two digits should be between '01' and '12'.
The month cannot be earlier than '201605'.
A bigstack month cannot not be earlier than '201606' and cannot be later than the (current month - 1).
A submodel month cannot not be earlier than '201605' and cannot be later than the (current month - 2).
